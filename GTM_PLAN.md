# Zimplifyed.ai — Sales & Marketing Plan (GTM)

Executes `VISION_1B.md` §7. Companion to `PRODUCT_PLAN.md` / `SELF_SERVE_PLAN.md`. This is the operating plan: positioning, channels, sales motions, budgets, targets, and the quarter-by-quarter calendar to hit **$1M ARR (2027) → $10M (2028) → $100M (2030)**.

---

## 1. Positioning & message

**Category we create:** "Export OS" — not CRM, not ERP, not a docs tool. One sentence:

> **Zimplifyed runs your entire export business — from buyer inquiry to money in the bank — with AI doing the paperwork.**

**Message hierarchy by persona:**
| Persona | Pain (their words) | Lead message |
|---|---|---|
| Founder/owner (2–20 staff) | "I'm the CRM, the CHA-chaser, and the accountant" | *Know your true margin on every order — and let AI do the documents.* |
| Merchant trader | "One wrong invoice line and the LC bounces" | *Every document cross-checked by AI before it leaves your office.* |
| Export ops manager | Deadline terror: LUT renewal, e-BRC, cert expiries | *Nothing expires, nothing gets missed. Zimplifyed watches everything.* |
| First-time exporter | "I got an export inquiry — now what?" | *Your first export order, guided step-by-step.* (the wedge segment) |

**Proof-first rules:** every claim backed by a number (docs generated, error rates, hours saved); demo the doc-set generation in the first 90 seconds of any pitch; price anchored against intermediary spend ("your CHA charges ₹4,000 per shipment for data entry"), never against software.

**Naming/brand hygiene (do now):** the product spells itself `Zimplifyed.ai`; secure zimplified.* misspelling domains + social handles, one logo/color system across app, PDFs, tracking links — buyer-facing artifacts are ads (VISION_1B §5).

## 2. ICP & segmentation

- **ICP-1 (bullseye, now):** Indian merchant exporters & small manufacturer-exporters, textiles/home-linen, 2–20 staff, ₹2–50Cr turnover, ≥2 shipments/mo, WhatsApp-run. Buyer = founder. Sales cycle: days.
- **ICP-2 (2027H2):** same profile, all verticals (spices, engineering, handicrafts, agro, pharma-adjacent).
- **ICP-3 (2028):** export consultants & CA firms managing 10–100 exporter clients (channel, not just customer).
- **ICP-4 (2029+):** overseas buyers/importers sourcing from India+ (supplier-enablement motion).
- **Disqualify early:** pure domestic traders (park in free tier), >500-employee enterprises (until 2030), one-shipment-a-year hobbyists.

## 3. Channel plan (ranked by expected CAC efficiency)

### 3.1 SEO/AEO/GEO — the compounding engine (start month 1)
Replicate the anabyn playbook on SaaS-intent keywords:
- **Programmatic pages:** {product/HS chapter} × {destination country} × {job}: "documents required to export towels to UAE", "RoDTEP rate for HS 6302", "LC checklist for Saudi buyers". Target 2,000+ pages by end-2027, each with an AEO answer block, FAQ schema, and an embedded free tool.
- **Free tools as lead magnets (each = a landing page + email gate on export/save):** HS code finder · landed-cost/Incoterm calculator · RoDTEP/drawback calculator · LC discrepancy checker (upload LC → AI flags issues; the single best PQL signal we can get) · export-readiness quiz for first-timers · document checklist generator.
- **GEO (answer-engine optimization):** llms.txt, structured comparisons, citable stat pages ("State of Indian SMB Exports" annual report — original data once we have tenants) so ChatGPT/Perplexity/Gemini recommend us when exporters ask questions there. Exporters *do* ask AI tools compliance questions now; being the cited source is 2027's version of ranking #1.
- KPI: organic → signup ≥ 3%; tools → signup ≥ 10%.

### 3.2 WhatsApp & community (the India-native channel)
- **Zimplifyed Exporters Circle:** WhatsApp communities per vertical/region; daily value drops (rate alerts, DGFT circular summaries in plain language — AI-generated, human-checked). Community first, product mentions ≤1/week.
- **WhatsApp bot as top-of-funnel:** free "ask an export question" bot → great answers → "want this connected to your actual orders?" This doubles as L1 product exposure.
- DGFT-circular-summary broadcast list = the newsletter, on the channel exporters actually read.

### 3.3 Partnerships & channel sales (2027H2 →)
- **CA firms & export consultants (ICP-3):** 20% year-1 revenue share, multi-client console (SELF_SERVE_PLAN multi-org already supports it), co-branded onboarding. Target: 50 active partners by 2028 ≈ 1,500 orgs. This is the cheapest path to $10M.
- **Export Promotion Councils & FIEO:** member-benefit pricing, joint webinars ("GST/LUT renewal season" timed content), presence in their newsletters. Credibility > leads.
- **Ecosystem partners:** forwarders/CHAs get a free counterpart console (they benefit from clean data; they refer clients); banks' trade desks (they want digitized clients — co-marketing); trade-portal complementarity (IndiaMART gives leads, we run the deal — "what happens after the inquiry" positioning, not competition).

### 3.4 Events & field (small budget, high intent)
Trade fairs where exporters exhibit (Heimtextil India, IHGF Delhi Fair, vertical-specific): don't buy a booth year 1 — walk the floor, demo doc-generation on a phone at exhibitors' stalls, collect WhatsApp numbers. Booth from 2028. Regional exporter meetups (Karur, Tirupur, Panipat, Ludhiana, Moradabad, Jaipur clusters) — 20 founders + dinner + live demo; clusters talk, one cluster win cascades.

### 3.5 Paid (only after organic/PLG proves conversion, 2027H2)
Google search on high-intent commercial terms ("export documentation software") — tiny spend, watch CAC ≤ 6-month payback. Meta/YouTube remarketing on tool users. LinkedIn only for ICP-4 buyer-side later. Never paid-first: our LTV at $50/mo doesn't survive undisciplined CAC.

### 3.6 Product-led loops (free, always-on)
Powered-by branding on tracking links/quote pages/PDFs (removed on paid) · vendor-portal invitations convert vendors to tenants · referral program (1 month free or AI-action credits both sides) · public template gallery (SEO + activation).

## 4. Sales motion by stage

### Stage 1 — Founder-led + PLG ($0→1M, 2026–27)
No salespeople. Founder does 5 demos/week (learning > closing). Self-serve checkout handles the rest. **PQL definition:** org with ≥2 users AND (≥3 quotes OR 1 doc-set generated) in 14 days → founder WhatsApps them personally. Onboarding = "white-glove by default": 30-min setup call offered to every signup (it's the research budget).

### Stage 2 — Inside sales on PQLs ($1→10M, 2027–28)
2 AEs (Tirupur/Karur-fluent Tamil + Hindi-belt) working PQLs and partner-sourced leads only — no cold outbound to SMBs (it doesn't pay at this ACV). Sales cycle target <14 days, single-call close with a live doc-set demo on *their* real shipment (ask them to bring one invoice). 1 partner manager for the CA/consultant channel. Success/onboarding: 1 per 300 paying orgs; onboarding in Hindi/Tamil/Telugu.

### Stage 3 — Segmented ($10→100M, 2029–30)
- SMB stays PLG + inside sales.
- **Mid-market/trading houses:** 3–5 field AEs, multi-entity + custom-role pitch, ACV $5–20k.
- **Fintech attach team:** payments/financing/insurance modules sold into the existing base (attach motion, not acquisition — the cheapest revenue in the company; VISION_1B engine 3).
- **Buyer-side/supplier-enablement:** 1–2 strategic AEs selling to overseas buyers who then mandate us to 50–200 suppliers each. One closed buyer = a quarter of SMB pipeline. Land via the compliance angle (CBAM/traceability evidence).
- Country-pack launches (Bangladesh, Vietnam…) each get: localized site + 200 programmatic pages + 2 local partners + 1 community, before any local hire.

## 5. Pricing & packaging as GTM (align w/ SELF_SERVE_PLAN §4)
- Free tier is deliberately generous on *viewing/CRM*, gated on *AI outcomes* (doc-sets, agent tasks) — the value metric matches willingness to pay and scales with customer success, not seat-counting games.
- Per-outcome pricing (₹/doc-set) is the pitch weapon: directly comparable to CHA data-entry fees. Publish the comparison table.
- Annual-prepay discount 2 months free (cash flow + retention). INR pricing, UPI/netbanking via Razorpay for India, Stripe elsewhere. GST invoices day one — CAs check this before approving the spend.

## 8. Funnel targets & metrics

**Model to $1M ARR (avg $80/org/mo blended):** need ~1,050 paying orgs by end-2027 → at 8% free→paid and 70% signup→org (SELF_SERVE_PLAN §9): ~13k signups/yr ≈ 1,100/mo by H2-2027. Sourced ~50% organic/tools, 25% community/WhatsApp, 15% partners, 10% referral/loops.

Weekly dashboard: signups by source → org-created % → activation (first quote 24h) → PQL rate → free→paid % → MRR, NRR, logo churn (<2.5%/mo SMB), CAC payback, tool-page conversion, community size, partner-sourced pipeline %. **North star: weekly active orgs generating ≥1 revenue-linked artifact (quote/doc-set/invoice).**

## 10. Risks & counters
- Programmatic SEO saturates or Google volatility → GEO + community + partners are 50% of sourcing by design, never >50% one channel.
- SMB churn from seasonality → pause plan (not cancel), annual prepay push, agent value that runs even in slow season (compliance renewals).
- Partners demand exclusivity → never grant geographic exclusivity; tier by performance instead.
- Copycat undercuts on price → sell the guarantee + accuracy record (VISION_1B §3), not the price; per-outcome pricing is hard to copy without the eval corpus.
- Founder demo bottleneck → record the perfect 8-min demo early; every page embeds it.
