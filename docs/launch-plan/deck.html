01 The thesis

We don't ship features. We ship departments.

An Indian exporter with three people does the work of eight departments. Zimplifyed replaces the missing five with AI departments — each one a workflow where AI does the expert labor and the human's only job is to approve, correct, or escalate.

The workflow is the wedge

Fintech, marketplace and per-outcome pricing only work because the department workflows capture the data. We never ship monetization ahead of the workflow that feeds it — the data is the moat.

Trust before autonomy

Every AI action is metered, cited, approval-gated and feedback-captured. Autonomy rises only as measured accuracy earns it. AI never files, sends, or pays externally without an explicit human tap.

WhatsApp is a first-class UI

For our core user the web app is the back office; WhatsApp is where the business actually runs. The unified inbox starts there — but email and web are peers, so no single channel is the spine.

02 The product

Eight departments, one approval tap each.

Every capability maps to a department a small exporter would otherwise staff. If it doesn't replace hours of a named role's work, it's cut. HR and a general ledger are deliberately out — we sync to Tally, we don't rebuild it.

03 The autonomy contract

"Minimal human intervention" is a measured promotion, not a promise.

A workflow moves up a level only when its acceptance-without-edit rate clears 60% over 100+ instances and the money- or compliance-guardrail owner signs off. Three rejections in a week auto-demotes it.

LevelMeaningWhere it applies
L0Never autonomousAI preps, a human executes outside the product — government filings (GST, ICEGATE, DGFT), payments, LC acceptance. Permanent.
L1Draft & approveAI drafts; explicit human approval before any external effect — all outbound comms, quote/doc release, RFQ broadcast, vendor award.
L2Auto with review queueAI acts inside the tenant's own data; human reviews by exception — extraction, high-confidence expense posting, lead logging, cost-sheet drafts.
L3Autonomous agentsMulti-step with a policy budget, post-hoc audit — follow-up cadences, expiry-renewal chains. Gated on the event stream + earned acceptance.

The v2 keystone — the Action Queue — is how this becomes a UI: one queue of every AI-proposed action across departments. The founder's day becomes the queue plus the daily brief.
